Six entirely product-metaphor concepts — grounded in what Lotris actually does. Every mark tells a story about IT ops, SLA performance, and the moment data surfaces into clarity.

Recommended Direction
Concept A The Surface

Directly visualises the tagline: "Where performance surfaces." Below the line = raw unstructured data/noise. Above the line = organised, rising metrics. The mark is instantly understood, scalable, and unique. The L-form is embedded in the negative space between the line and the tallest bar.
